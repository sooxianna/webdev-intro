There are many options for housing your bunny indoors, including puppy pens, bunny condos, large cages, or simply a bunny proofed room. We will discuss all the options so you can decide what is best for your living arrangement.

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha (along with the hare and the pika). Oryctolagus cuniculus includes the European rabbit species and its descendants, the world's 305 breeds of domestic rabbit.

Male rabbits are called bucks; females are called does. An older term for an adult rabbit is coney, while rabbit once referred only to the young animals.
